About Me

I am an undergraduate at Barnard College, where I am majoring in physics and astronomy and minoring in Russian language and mathematics. I am also an undergraduate researcher at the American Museum of Natural History, where I study brown dwarfs with the Brown Dwarfs of New York City research group.

My research aims to characterize the atmospheres of brown dwarfs, which are exoplanet analogs. I am studying cloud-driven variability on L and T-type brown dwarfs by studying single epoch spectra obtained using the SpeX spectrograph built at the Institute for Astronomy for the NASA Infrared Telescope Facility
